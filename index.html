Instructions for Candidates

  1. Candidates must carry this Admit Card to the examination hall on all days.
  2. All candidates must report to the school before 8:45 AM. The school gate will be closed at 8:45 AM. No candidate will be permitted to enter the examination hall after this time under any circumstances.
  3. The examination will commence at 9:30 AM.
  4. Question papers will be distributed 10 minutes before the start of the examination (at 9:20 AM).
  5. No electronic devices (mobiles, smart watches, etc.) are allowed in the examination hall.
  6. Candidates should not write anything on the admit card except for the required signatures.
